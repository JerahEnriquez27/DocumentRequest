The ClearDocs is an online platform that lets users quickly submit requests without visiting an office.

Purpose:
- Allow users to request documents quickly and conveniently.
- Help officials manage, review, and process requests efficiently.
- Improve transparency by letting users clearly see the status of their requests.
Users can submit document requests anytime, and officials can track and update them promptly, ensuring faster and more organized service.
